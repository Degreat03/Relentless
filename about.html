About us

Relentless Prayer Force

Relentless Prayer Force (RPF) is an intercessory movement established by divine instruction, not by human recruitment. Participation is not by invitation, publicity, or addition—it is by divine leading. Those aligned with this work are drawn by conviction, spiritual burden, and alignment with its mandate. RPF exists as a disciplined prayer force committed to sustained intercession, with a central focus on the propagation of the Gospel across the nations. This is not a casual prayer gathering, but a consecrated movement of men and women devoted to tarrying in God’s presence regardless of personal circumstance, comfort, or convenience. We are persuaded that God is raising a remnant—men and women who desire nothing but the fulfillment of His will, who worship Him in every sphere of life, and who contend in prayer until His purposes become visible realities in nations, communities, and individual lives. The scriptural anchor of this movement is Jude 1:3: "Beloved, when I gave all diligence to write unto you of the common salvation, it was needful for me to write unto you, and exhort you that ye should earnestly contend for the faith which was once delivered unto the saints." Relentless Prayer Force is not defined by numbers, platforms, or visibility, but by obedience, consistency, and spiritual endurance. It is a call to those who will not relent until God’s purposes are established on the earth.

Our Vision

To see a global remnant of men and women wholly devoted to God, contending through prayer until His will is established in every nation, community, and life, and the Gospel advances unhindered across the earth.

Our Mission

To function as a disciplined intercessory prayer force, divinely raised to engage in sustained, intentional, and God-aligned prayer, contending earnestly for the faith and prioritizing God’s redemptive agenda above all else.

Our Mandate

Guiding Principles, Driving Purpose

  • Engage in intentional, sustained intercession aligned with God’s purposes.
  • Tarry in God’s presence with perseverance and spiritual discipline.
  • Contend earnestly for the faith once delivered to the saints.
  • Stand in the gap for nations, communities, and individuals.
  • Live lives of consecration that prioritize God’s will above personal interests.

Who RPF Is For?

Relentless Prayer Force is for believers who:

  • Carry a burden for God’s purposes on the earth.
  • Are drawn to a life of sustained intercession.
  • Desire depth, discipline, and spiritual endurance.
  • Are willing to prioritize God’s agenda above personal comfort.
