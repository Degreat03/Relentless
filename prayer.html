Divine Intercession

Prayer of thanksgiving

“Give thanks to the Lord, for He is good; His steadfast love endures forever. We bless You, O Lord, for the gift of life, For daily provision, for peace, and for joy. For Your comfort in times of sorrow, Let our hearts remember Your wonders, In Jesus’ name, Amen.”

(Inspired by Psalm 107:1, 1 Thessalonians 5:18, and Psalm 103:1-2)

Prayer for the Nation

"Heavenly Father, We lift our nation into Your hands, Guide our leaders with wisdom, our people with unity, And protect us with Your steadfast love. Bring justice where there is oppression, Peace where there is conflict, And hope where hearts are weary, May our nation walk in Your light, Amen."

(Inspired by Psalm 33:12, Proverbs 11:14, 1 Timothy 2:1-2, Psalm 82:3-4)

Prayer for the Church

Heavenly Father, We lift Your Church into Your hands, Guide its leaders with wisdom, its members with love, And protect it with Your unfailing presence. Strengthen us to proclaim Your truth, Serve our communities with compassion, Amen.

(Inspired by Ephesians 4:11-13, Colossians 1:9-12, Matthew 5:14-16, Psalm 133:1)

Prayer for the Family

Heavenly Father, We lift our families into Your loving hands, Guide our parents with wisdom, our children with understanding, And bind us together with Your steadfast love. Protect us from harm, strengthen our hearts in patience and kindness, And fill our homes with peace, joy, and faith. Amen.

(Inspired by Joshua 24:15, Proverbs 22:6, Psalm 127:1, Ephesians 6:1-4)
